Welcome to Metzer Farms

We are a family-owned farm and hatchery that has been in business since 1972.

We have mastered nutrition, breeding, selection, incubation, and hatching to ensure our customers receive the highest quality of day-old birds. We specialize in waterfowl, hatching 19 breeds of ducks and 16 breeds of geese. We also hatch French Pearl Guineas and Ringneck Pheasants, as well as three strains of meat chickens.

Need Help?

At Metzer Farms, we believe in more than just providing top-quality ducks and geese; we’re dedicated to ensuring your experience with us is as delightful and rewarding as the company of our waterfowl. Our friendly customer service team is here to help with every step—from choosing the perfect breeds to offering expert care advice.

Your satisfaction and the well-being of your feathered friends are our top priorities. Reach out to us; we’re excited to be a part of your waterfowl journey!
